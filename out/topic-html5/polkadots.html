How to create polka dots with the wet-on-wet icing technique.

Polka dots are an easy way to use the wet-on-wet icing technique.
  1. Squeeze piping bag gently to drop spots of colored icing into flooded base icing.
  2. Release pressure on piping bag while lifting away from cookie.
  3. Let crust and dry.
