Welcome!
Decorating sugar cookies with royal icing is a challenge. However, the outcome is often beautiful (and delicious!). Custom, hand-decorated cookies are expensive, so learning to decorate on your own can be very rewarding!
Necessary Tools
You will need different tools for making, coloring, and decorating the icing.
Making Your Royal Icing
Royal icing is used to decorate sugar cookies because it dries quickly and its consistency is nice to work with when decorating.
Coloring and Thinning Your Royal Icing
Royal icing is colored by mixing small amounts of water and food gel coloring into the icing bowl. At times, a few drops of water makes the difference between too thick, too thin, or just right.
